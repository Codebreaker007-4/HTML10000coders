At the stroke of midnight, marking the conclusion of the wildly popular virtual reality game Yggdrasil, Momonga, a skilled wizard and leader of the dark guild Ainz Ooal Gown, opts to remain logged in until the game's final seconds. However, he's shocked to discover that he's still fully cognitive even as the servers begin to shut down. To his amazement, the non-player characters have morphed into unique personalities! In response to this unforeseen turn of events, Momonga rallies his faithful followers while taking charge of this new world. His primary objective is to figure out what caused this inexplicable phenomenon and if others are experiencing the same occurrence.
